Purchasing a land


The most important point to note is verifying the clarity of the plan.


Purchasing from a person


Benefits
Get a land for reasonable price.
And there are some customers who prefer calm areas to live.


Drawbacks
Owner can mislead the customer easily.
Customer may have no idea about the area.

A solution for the drawbacks is to ask from people who live in the area about the history of land.

Purchasing from a land sale


You should check "veethi reka sahathikaya" for part of the land which going to buy. Because the permission for whole land will not ensure the validity of the the part of land to make a building
Buyer have to consider about price, waterflow,drainage electricity supply etc.

Benefits
people are in same social level
Easy to take loans
Reliabilty is high


Drawbacks
Usually high prices
May be a crowded surrounding

You can now bid for lands published in our bidding section. These bids consists of land which are to be sold offered by reputed land sales companies.
