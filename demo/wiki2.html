Hiring a Service Provider

 

The factors that you need to consider when selecting a Service Provider for the tasks.

1. Competence- The competence of a Service Provider will be the basic requirement for any type of Service Provider. Some of the Service Providers should be qualified such as a qualified architect. The technical expertise of some Service Providers such as electricians are critical when building the house.

2. Experience- More the Service Provider is experienced it is the better.

3. Past work- If a Service Provider has worked in your area previously he will know more regarding the conditions of the area such as geography, weather, etc. Therefore selecting a Service Provider who knows about your area would be helpful.

4. Opinions given by his customers- This may be the best source of selecting a Service Provider. At present normally the customers rely on a word-of-mouth recommendation of some one to select a Service Provider. If the recommender makes an error of judgement and if the customer does not get another opinion of a different person he will get mislead. You will realise the importance of baas.lk considering this because in here more customers could post comments and make reviews so it is easier to understand who is the best Service Provider for your purpose.
